Who Can Donate Blood?

National Blood Transfusion Service - Donor eligibility, travel deferrals and guidelines

Basic Donor Eligibility Criteria

  • Aged 18-60 years. (First-time donors: up to 55 years.)
  • Minimum 4 months between donations.
  • Weight above 50 kg.
  • Haemoglobin above 12.5 g/dL.
  • Not pregnant; free from serious medical illnesses.
  • Free from "Risk Behaviors".
  • Bring National Identity Card or valid ID.

Travel-related Deferrals

Malaria-endemic country travel: Donors are deferred for 3 years from the date of return.

Other foreign travel: Donors are deferred for 3 months from the date of return.

If unsure about your travel status, ask NBTS staff or contact us before donation.

Before Donating Blood

  • Have a main meal within 4 hours.
  • Drink plenty of fluids.
  • Avoid alcohol.
  • Rest at least 6 hours the previous night.

After Donating Blood

  • Rest for at least 20 minutes.
  • Drink more fluids in the next 4 hours.
  • Keep plaster on for about 12 hours.
  • Avoid heavy lifting for 24 hours.
